Cory Van Duyn, PSM III

Certified Scrum.org Professional Scrum Master level three (PSM III) and Project Management Institute Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP) with over 7 years of experience cultivating high-performing agile software development and business teams.

My approach is built on enhancing three essential pillars: purpose, capability, and support. I coach on a foundation of servant leadership, accountability, and clear communication. I thrive in building teams across diverse groups, driving business process enhancements, and developing products that deliver data-driven solutions that fuel business growth.

My approach is centered on finding better ways of working by integrating modern product development principles with a customer-obsessed focus. I believe that a successful organization starts with building strong relationships and truly understanding the unique needs of the teams involved, making sure we own the outcome every step of the way.

Before suggesting changes to team structures or processes, I take the time to assess the specific dynamics and needs of the environment. By fostering a diverse and inclusive culture, I ensure that all perspectives are valued, creating solutions that are tailored, sustainable, and agile. Trust and collaboration are key, and I work closely with teams to drive improvements in both efficiency and engagement, ensuring we succeed better together.

When it comes to team operations, I focus on removing obstacles early and promoting shared understanding. I encourage a growth mindset where teams continuously learn and evolve. I believe in shipping to learn - delivering results iteratively and refining our development based on feedback to maximize customer value.

This approach builds alignment, keeps teams focused on what matters most, and helps them adapt to change.


By setting clear, realistic goals and fostering a shared sense of purpose, teams become empowered to work more effectively, enhance collaboration, and fully engage with their mission.
